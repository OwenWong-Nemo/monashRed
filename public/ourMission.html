Our Mission

Why We Exist

We are driven by the belief that no family should experience the tragedy of losing a child to preventable causes. Our mission is to reduce Sudden Unexpected Death in Infancy (SUDI) through innovation, empathy, and culturally inclusive design.

Mission Statement

To design and deliver a safe, user-friendly, and culturally respectful infant sleeping device that supports healthy sleep practices across diverse communities.

Our Focus Areas

  • Reduce SUDI-related infant mortality through evidence-based design and education.
  • Commercialization & Marketing to ensure accessibility, affordability, and desirability.
  • Community Collaboration to co-design with cultural leaders, caregivers, and healthcare providers.

Our Key Objectives

  • Create a product that is user-friendly, intuitive, and adaptable for daily life.
  • Design with cultural sensitivity in mind to honor varying infant sleep practices.
  • Develop a unique product that stands out visually and functionally.
  • Engage with communities to build trust and receive feedback.
  • Ensure the design is scalable and affordable across different income groups.
